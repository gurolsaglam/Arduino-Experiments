Pretty straightforward, simple read-report module, reads the value and reports it.

Connect the lower voltage side to the GND and the higher voltage side to the VCC.

Connect S pin to the analog input pin,
- pin to GND in Arduino,
+ pin does nothing, just leave it floating (not connected).
